# Code of Conduct

Esheria's public package community is expected to be professional, respectful,
and safe. Be constructive, assume good faith, respect privacy and licensing
boundaries, and avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, or disclosure of
another person's confidential information.

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and interactions
that violate these expectations. Report conduct concerns privately through
https://esheria.ai/contact. Maintainers will handle reports with appropriate
confidentiality and may restrict participation when necessary to protect the
community.
